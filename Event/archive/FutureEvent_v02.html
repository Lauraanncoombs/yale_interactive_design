In celebration of Spring and the showing of Hugh the Hunter at Sundance this year, I would like to invite you to a special hunting themed BBQ** followed by a screening of the 10-minute short film, Hugh the Hunter.

My artistic practice explores shared histories despite disparate appearances. I utilize natural signifiers such as bark, clothing, feathers, and hair to subvert and add varied perspective to concepts of identity I consider socially ubiquitous.
